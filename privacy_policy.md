# Privacy Policy for Where Is Kenny

**Last updated: March 10, 2026**

## Overview
Where Is Kenny is a tourist and art discovery app that helps users locate a sculpture installed in Tromsø, Norway. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
The app uses your device's location to help you navigate to the sculpture. Location data is processed on your device and is not stored on our servers.

### Advertising
The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by our advertising partner (Google AdMob) to serve ads.

## Data Retention
We do not store any personal data on our servers. Location data is processed locally on your device.

## Children's Privacy
This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on this page.
